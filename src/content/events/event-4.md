Our monthly reading group continues with a text that challenges everything we think we know about the university, study, and intellectual life.

## About the Book

*The Undercommons* asks: what does it mean to study together outside the structures that claim to own knowledge? Harney and Moten propose a fugitive practice of study — one that happens in the undercommons, in the spaces below and between institutions.

## How It Works

- Read the book (or as much as you can) before the meeting
- Copies available at the shop at a reduced price
- No expertise required — just curiosity and good faith
- Tea and snacks provided
