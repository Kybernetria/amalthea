Housing is a right, not a commodity. But knowing your rights is the first step to defending them.

## What You'll Learn

- Your legal rights as a tenant in this state
- How to document landlord violations
- How to organize your building
- How to connect with the Rivertown Tenants Union for ongoing support

## Who Should Come

Anyone who rents. Whether you're dealing with a specific issue or just want to be prepared, this workshop is for you. Bring your lease if you have questions about specific clauses.

Materials provided. Childcare available — let us know in advance. Spanish interpretation available.
