People ask us sometimes why we don't sell online. Why we don't have an e-commerce store, a shipping department, a social media marketing strategy. The answer is simple: we're not a business. We're a project.

## The Long Game

Independent bookstores aren't competing with Amazon. We're doing something fundamentally different. We're building a space where people can gather, browse without algorithms, and discover books through conversation — not through surveillance-driven recommendation engines.

When you buy a book here, you're supporting a worker-owned cooperative. You're keeping rent paid on a community space. You're funding the reading groups, the childcare during events, the free coffee.

## Slow Commerce

We believe in slow commerce. Come in. Take your time. Read the first chapter standing in the aisle. Ask us what we've been reading. Leave without buying anything — that's fine too. The door is always open.

The point was never to move units. The point is to build a commons.
