We've just unpacked a beautiful shipment of new titles that we're thrilled to share with you. From reprinted labor histories to brand-new poetry chapbooks from local writers, the shelves are brimming.

## Highlights This Month

**"Mutual Aid: Building Solidarity During This Crisis (and the Next)"** — Dean Spade's essential guide to organizing care in community. If you haven't read it yet, now's the time.

**"Braiding Sweetgrass"** — Robin Wall Kimmerer weaves together indigenous wisdom and scientific knowledge in this gorgeous meditation on reciprocity with the living world.

**"The Dispossessed"** — Ursula K. Le Guin's anarchist utopia classic, back in a new edition with an introduction by China Miéville.

## Zine Corner

We've also got a fresh batch of zines from the regional zine fair, covering everything from tenants' rights to foraging guides to hand-drawn comics about collective living. Come browse — they're in the basket by the front window, as always.

All titles are available at the shop. We don't do online orders — come in, browse, stay for tea.
