Poetry & Resistance: An Open Mic Night

🕐 19:00 – 21:30 📍 Main Floor 👤 The Collective

Join us for an evening of poetry and spoken word in the warm glow of the shop floor. We’ll have the chairs arranged in a circle, the kettle on, and the mic open.

Format

  • 7:00 PM — Doors open, tea and snacks
  • 7:30 PM — Featured reader: Maria Santos (local poet, author of Salt & Sediment)
  • 8:00 PM — Open mic begins (5-minute slots, sign up at the door)
  • 9:30 PM — Close

Bring your words, bring your friends. First-timers especially welcome. This is not a competition — it’s a gathering.
